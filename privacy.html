Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: March 2026

1 Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how 1322 Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects user data. We are committed to respecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2 Data We Collect

The Bot collects only the minimum data necessary for its functionality:

  • Server IDs — to store per-server configuration
  • User IDs — for guild tag tracking, warnings, and moderation logs
  • Moderation records — warnings, bans, kicks, and timeouts
  • Guild tag status — which users carry the server's clan tag

3 Data We Do NOT Collect Important

We never collect the following:

  • Message content (except transiently when message logging is enabled)
  • Personal information such as email addresses or phone numbers
  • Payment or financial information
  • Location data

4 Data Storage

All data is stored locally in JSON files on the server where the Bot is hosted. We do not use any third-party databases or cloud storage services.

5 Third Parties

Your data is never shared, sold, or rented to any third party. The Bot operates solely through the Discord API and is subject to Discord's own Privacy Policy.

6 Data Deletion

To request deletion of your server's data, contact us through our Discord server. Removing the Bot from your server will stop any further data collection.
